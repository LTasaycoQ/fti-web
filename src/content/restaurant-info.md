Estas varían de acuerdo a la región y tienen diferentes texturas y colores.     contentEn: |

They are varied according to region and have diverse textures and colors. For example, Yucca root, native to the crescent rain forest near Cusco and Machu Picchu, is a root that can be served as a side dish to seafood or meat. In addition, some of our dishes are prepared with the infamous maca root, which is a well-known source of energetic nutrients.
